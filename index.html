Gloria Kimbwala

Gloria was recently named one of the top 10 women in Fintech byFintech Ranking. Gloria currently works at Square as a University Tech Evangelist where she attends hackathons and helps students with the tools necessary to get into tech. Gloria is also the leads Square Code Camp and STEPS where she helps introduce historically excluded groups get into tech. Her non-traditional path into tech includes a Bachelors Degree in Environmental Studies and a Master's degree in Computer Science. Gloria is an advocate for inclusion of all types into tech including diversity of age, ability, race, gender, and life experiences. She is passionate about teaching tech to anyone who will listen. She started teaching her 6 year old son to program when he was 2!
